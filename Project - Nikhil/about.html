About Us

Javateas is a gourmet coffee cafe serving daily fresh roasted coffee and light fares in three welcoming and/or community-oriented atmospheres!

Javateas and our three locations provide a neighborhood meeting place that serves the freshest tasting and highest quality coffee possible while staying committed to a speed-of-service and a knowledgeable and friendly staff. This is achieved in an atmosphere that invites guests to relax from their busy day in a cafe where free wireless internet, live music, and comfortable and private seating arrangements are available.

Visit Javateas today to discover the difference of quality food, drinks, and service. Javateas, your only real choice.
